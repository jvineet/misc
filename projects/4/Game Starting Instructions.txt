To play run the ScrabbleGui file in the location "edu.cmu.cs.cs214.hw4.gui"

A small start game window will apear. Enter the names of upto 4 players that 
will play together. Then click on Start scrabble button. Each player has his/her 
own window that is updated as soon as a player makes move. 

A word tile is placed on the board by clicking on it in the rack and then clicking on
the board location where the tile should be placed on the desired square in the board. 
Once the move is made click on play. Else click skip to skip the turn.
